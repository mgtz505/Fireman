Welcome to Fireman - A Game for Two Players

Thank you for joining the General Assembly SEI Volunteer Fire Department!

A very important data center in Menlo Park is on fire! To put it out, we need to deduce the mystery word that was just selected by Player One.

Each of the yellow boxes represents a letter that must be deduced by Player Two.

If you, Player Two, can guess all of the letters in time, we can save all the vulnerable servers!

We only have a little amount of time before the servers are destroyed. Unfortunately we don't know exactly how long we have... but maybe the word length can give us a hint....
